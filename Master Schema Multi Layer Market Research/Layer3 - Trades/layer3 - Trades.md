Layer 3 — Trades
Trade Events, Engine Interaction Points, Execution Behavior & Structural Context

Purpose
Layer 3 records every trade with full structural context — not just entry/exit.
It captures:

Market identity

Entry & exit price

Profit/Loss

Direction (Buy/Sell)

Structural reason for entry

Structural reason for exit

Emotional state

Spike events during the trade

Actor behavior

Book behavior

Dump & recovery signatures

Time in trade

Freeform notes

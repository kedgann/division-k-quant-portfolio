Layer 1 — Markets
Market List, Engine Identity, Structural Classification & Tracking Scope


Purpose
Layer 1 defines the full market universe your system tracks.
This is where each market is assigned:

its engine identity (synthetic / natural / hybrid)

its structural classification

its tracking scope

its relevance to downstream layers

its expected behavior profile

Layer 1 is the entry point of your entire architecture.
Every higher layer (2 through 10) depends on the market definitions, identities, and classifications established here.

This layer ensures your system knows what engines exist, how they behave, and which structural rules apply to each one.

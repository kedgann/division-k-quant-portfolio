🔹 Purpose
Layer 2 captures daily structural behavior for each market, compressing the entire trading day into a readable engine snapshot.

It tracks:

End‑day high & low

Peak volume time

Volume sequences (compressed)

Natural vs synthetic spike counts

Largest spike sizes

Actor pressure

Engine stability

Notes
Daily logs are valuable because they show:

how often an engine enters synthetic pressure

how frequently natural spikes appear

whether the engine is trending toward instability

how volume compresses or expands across the day

whether actors are present, absent, or coordinating

how spike size evolves over time

how synthetic memory reinforcement appears across days

whether the engine is drifting, coiling, or preparing for ignition

Layer 2 proves you’re not just collecting data — you’re interpreting engine behavior and building a structural timeline of each market’s evolution.
